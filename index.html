01 — Content strategy & live coverage

AFROPUNK Brasil

Content strategy and copywriting for AFROPUNK's São Paulo debut. Editorial planning and live social coverage for a festival that brought together Ludmilla, Amaarae, and over a dozen Brazilian artists — and sponsors including Heineken, Coca-Cola, and Banco do Brasil.

ClientAFROPUNK / IDW Company
Year2024
ServicesContent strategy, copywriting, live social coverage, brand alignment

The work

AFROPUNK is a global platform for Black culture, expression, and community. In 2024, it expanded across Brazil for the first time and I was invited to contribute with content strategy and copywriting for the São Paulo Experience edition.

I worked on editorial planning and live coverage for social media, crafting texts and videos that captured the spirit of the festival: powerful, political, and deeply human. Working closely with key sponsors — Banco do Brasil, Heineken, Coca-Cola, Red Bull, White Horse, Club Social, and Avatim — I helped align brand narratives with the soul of the event.

AFROPUNK's first-ever edition in the city brought together international sensation Amaarae with national powerhouses like Ludmilla, Anelis Assumpção, DJ Nyack, Rachel Reis, and Yago Oproprio — blending local and global influences into an unforgettable cultural experience.

02 — Content strategy & storymaking

Final Level Co

Led content strategy and editorial planning for Final Level, a Brazilian media company focused on creators and gaming culture. On Favela Gaming, served as storymaker for one of the country's most significant grassroots esports projects.

ClientFinal Level Co
Year2024–2025
ServicesEditorial strategy, content planning, social media, storymaking

The work

Final Level Co is a Brazilian media company at the intersection of gaming, creator culture, and youth identity. As Content Strategist, I led editorial planning and content strategy for the brand and its creator community.

On Favela Gaming — one of Brazil's most culturally significant grassroots esports initiatives — I served as storymaker, responsible for capturing and communicating the narrative behind the project: teams, players, community, and the broader cultural weight of competitive gaming coming from the favela.

In 6 months, the brand grew its Instagram following by 25% with just 10 posts per month — proof that focused editorial strategy consistently outperforms volume.

03 — Journalism & SEO content

Unilever Food Solutions

Educational and inspirational content for food service professionals — chefs, restaurant owners, operators. Developed the Future Menus 2024 Brazil edition and built a consistent SEO strategy around gastronomy, nutrition, and culinary technique.

ClientUnilever Food Solutions via Green Park Content
Year2022–2024
ServicesSEO writing, journalism, community management, content strategy

The work

Unilever Food Solutions is the B2B unit of Unilever, serving the food service sector with brands like Hellmann's, Knorr Professional, Maizena, and Arisco. The audience is highly specialized — professional chefs and restaurant operators — which required both technical accuracy and genuine culinary passion in every piece.

I developed educational articles with strong SEO strategy, covering topics from mise en place to sous chef roles, Brazilian gastronomy, and seasonal cooking. I also contributed to the Future Menus 2024 Brazil edition — a flagship publication featuring an interview with chef Thaís Gimenez on seasonality in Brazilian cuisine.

04 — Creative copywriting & social content

Enterogermina (Sanofi)

Developed creative and educational social media content for a healthcare brand that needed to be both medically accurate and genuinely fun. Tied seasonal cultural moments — like Rock in Rio — to product messaging in a way that felt organic, not forced.

ClientSanofi via Green Park Content
Year2022–2023
ServicesCreative copywriting, social content, community management

The work

Enterogermina is a Sanofi probiotic brand with strict healthcare communication regulations — every piece of content has to be medically accurate, legally compliant, and approved by the regulatory team. The brief was to make it creative and fun within those limits.

The strategy was to use educational content with a playful approach, leaning into cultural moments to make the brand feel present and human. In September, we tied the Rock in Rio season to gut health messaging — content that felt spontaneous and culturally aware rather than brand-pushed.

The approach worked because it respected the audience's intelligence: people interested in gut health are also people living cultural moments. The brand didn't need to fake relevance — it just needed to show up in the right context with the right tone.

01 — Content strategy & live coverage

AFROPUNK Brasil

Content strategy and copywriting for AFROPUNK's São Paulo debut. Editorial planning and live social coverage for a festival that brought together Ludmilla, Amaarae, and over a dozen Brazilian artists — and sponsors including Heineken, Coca-Cola, and Banco do Brasil.

ClientAFROPUNK / IDW Company
Year2024
ServicesContent strategy, copywriting, live social coverage, brand alignment

The work

AFROPUNK is a global platform for Black culture, expression, and community. In 2024, it expanded across Brazil for the first time and I was invited to contribute with content strategy and copywriting for the São Paulo Experience edition.

I worked on editorial planning and live coverage for social media, crafting texts and videos that captured the spirit of the festival: powerful, political, and deeply human. Working closely with key sponsors — Banco do Brasil, Heineken, Coca-Cola, Red Bull, White Horse, Club Social, and Avatim — I helped align brand narratives with the soul of the event without compromising its identity.

AFROPUNK's first-ever edition in the city brought together international sensation Amaarae with national powerhouses like Ludmilla, Anelis Assumpção, DJ Nyack, Rachel Reis, and Yago Oproprio — blending local and global influences into an unforgettable cultural experience.

02 — Content strategy & social media

Final Level Co

Joined as Social Media and promoted to Content Strategy Senior in 6 months. Responsible for the content presence of a company with millions of followers navigating a full brand repositioning — from gaming roots toward content tech.

ClientFinal Level Co
Year2024–2025
ServicesSocial media strategy, content production, editorial planning, event coverage

The work

Final Level Co is a Brazilian content tech company born in gaming culture and now operating across technology, entertainment, and youth identity. When I joined, the brand was in the middle of a strategic repositioning — and I took over its social media channels, which together reach millions of followers.

The impact of those first six months was significant enough that I was promoted from Social Media to Content Strategy Senior. In that role, I expanded scope to lead overall editorial strategy: defining voice, planning content architecture, and building systems that could sustain a brand in constant motion.

Final Level's client portfolio includes Google, Meta, TikTok, Disney, Samsung, Mercado Livre, Banco do Brasil, O Boticário, and Riot Games, among others. My work focused on Final Level's own brand — producing and strategizing content that positioned the company credibly within the creator economy and content tech space while honoring its gaming DNA.

Event coverage at CCXP, Gamescom, Youpix Summit, and Riot Games activations was part of the role — planning editorial frameworks before each event and managing live content production on the ground.

03 — Storymaking & content

Favela Gaming

Storymaker for one of Brazil's most culturally significant esports projects — a collaboration between Final Level Co, Gerando Falcões, and YouTube Gaming that brings competitive gaming to communities on the periphery.

PartnersFinal Level Co, Gerando Falcões, YouTube Gaming
Year2024–2025
ServicesStorymaking, content production, event coverage, social media

The work

Favela Gaming is a project born from the belief that competitive gaming belongs to everyone. A collaboration between Final Level Co, Gerando Falcões — Brazil's largest social impact organization — and YouTube Gaming, it creates pathways for young people from the periphery to enter the esports ecosystem.

As storymaker, I was responsible for capturing and communicating the human narrative behind the project: the players, their communities, the weight of what it means to compete at a high level when the system wasn't built for you. The work demanded both journalistic precision and genuine cultural sensitivity.

Coverage included live events, player interviews, and behind-the-scenes content that translated the spirit of Favela Gaming to broader audiences — without flattening or exoticizing the communities at its center.

04 — Journalism & SEO content

Unilever Food Solutions

Educational and inspirational content for food service professionals — chefs, restaurant owners, operators. Developed the Future Menus 2024 Brazil edition and grew the brand's Instagram by 25% in 6 months with just 10 posts per month.

ClientUnilever Food Solutions via Green Park Content
Year2022–2024
ServicesSEO writing, journalism, community management, content strategy

The work

Unilever Food Solutions is the B2B unit of Unilever, serving the food service sector with brands like Hellmann's, Knorr Professional, Maizena, and Arisco. The audience is highly specialized — professional chefs and restaurant operators — which required both technical accuracy and genuine culinary passion in every piece.

I developed educational articles with strong SEO strategy, covering topics from mise en place to sous chef roles, Brazilian gastronomy, and seasonal cooking. I also contributed to the Future Menus 2024 Brazil edition — a flagship annual publication featuring an interview with chef Thaís Gimenez on seasonality in Brazilian cuisine, with my byline on page 30.

The social media strategy we built together delivered 25% follower growth in just 6 months — with a deliberately restrained cadence of 10 posts per month. Focused editorial strategy, not volume.

05 — Creative copywriting & social content

Enterogermina (Sanofi)

Developed creative and educational social media content for a healthcare brand that needed to be both medically accurate and genuinely fun. Tied seasonal cultural moments — like Rock in Rio — to product messaging in a way that felt organic, not forced.

ClientSanofi via Green Park Content
Year2022–2023
ServicesCreative copywriting, social content, community management

The work

Enterogermina is a Sanofi probiotic brand with strict healthcare communication regulations — every piece of content has to be medically accurate, legally compliant, and approved by the regulatory team. The brief was to make it creative and fun within those limits.

The strategy was to use educational content with a playful approach, leaning into cultural moments to make the brand feel present and human. In September, we tied the Rock in Rio season to gut health messaging — content that felt spontaneous and culturally aware rather than brand-pushed.

The approach worked because it respected the audience's intelligence: people interested in gut health are also people living cultural moments. The brand didn't need to fake relevance — it just needed to show up in the right context with the right tone.

06 — Journalism & SEO

Taofeminino

SEO journalism and content production for one of Brazil's largest women's digital portals, part of the French group Aufeminin — present in Germany, the UK, the US, Vietnam, and beyond. Articles that went viral on Facebook with over 1,000 shares each.

ClientTaofeminino / Aufeminin Group
Year2016–2017
ServicesSEO journalism, content production, translation (EN/DE)

The work

Taofeminino was a Brazilian portal from the French group Aufeminin, focused on content representing the modern woman. It was my first major digital journalism experience — and where I learned that writing for the internet means understanding how people actually read, search, and share.

I produced SEO-driven journalism covering culture, diversity, feminism, and lifestyle. Several articles went viral on Facebook, reaching over 1,000 shares each — including pieces on African names for babies, Black women authors in Brazilian literature, and the internet phenomenon of Thor, the world's most beautiful cat.

The work also involved translation from English and German, which fed directly into my linguistic sensibility and my understanding of how tone, register, and cultural context shape the meaning of a text — not just its words.
